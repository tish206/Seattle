Community Resources

Hyde Shuttle

The Hyde Shuttle, offered by Sound Generations, provides door-to-door transportation services for senior citizens and older adults with disabilities throughout King County, including Seattle. This service operates during weekdays from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM and is tailored for trips to grocery stores, community centers, meal programs, and appointments like nail salons or local errands.

  • Door-to-Door Service: Seniors can be picked up from their home and dropped off at their destination.
  • Community-Oriented: Helps seniors stay connected with community events and errands beyond medical appointments.
  • Accessible Rides: Vans are ADA and wheelchair accessible, accommodating mobility aids like wheelchairs and scooters.
  • Cost: Free rides (donations accepted) ensure affordability.
  • Booking Process: Schedule rides by calling 206-727-6262.

Hyde Shuttle is a vital resource for keeping seniors active and engaged in their communities. If their hours or service area don’t meet your needs, explore local senior centers for supplemental programs that may offer additional flexibility.
